About Me

Being born in this generation, technology has played an important part of my life. It made learning more interactive and enjoyable. When my elementary school got Elmo boards my classroom had a competition to see who could do multiplication the fastest. The board allowed the teacher to share the computer screen to the whiteboard while being able to interact it with a electronic pen. This greatly helped me with my education because it was fun and was more entertaining than following along in an overhead projector.

I also like working with creative software tools like Adobe products since I am able to see and share my own creation. It's worthwhile and enjoyable to work and see my creations come to life Because of this, I aspire to become a software developer.
